How to play

Point and click to explore the room, find hidden items and solve riddles.
You can change the viewpoint by clicking on the point of interest on the screen.

[ About item ]

The items which you found are added to the item list, you can use the items by clicking on them.

The item detail window appears by clicking the magnifying glass mark (appear on the item list), you can examine the item closely and use it.
In the detail window, you can use another item on the enlarged item.

[ Save ]

You can save the game progress by clicking the "SAVE" button in the lower right of screen.
